Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh 2.0 represents a transformative vision for the nation, emphasizing technology-driven progress, entrepreneurship, and sustainable development. The country is leveraging its youthful population and growing digital infrastructure to foster innovation across industries.

Sustainability

Bangladesh 2.0 refers to the country's vision for progress, integrating sustainable development into its growth agenda. With a population exceeding 170 million, Bangladesh faces challenges like climate change, resource scarcity, and urbanization.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh's infrastructure has been a critical area of focus in its journey toward becoming a middle-income country. The nation's development efforts aim to improve transport, energy, communication, and urban infrastructure to support economic growth and sustainable development.

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh 2.0 reflects the nation's journey toward social, economic, and technological transformation. Over recent decades, Bangladesh has focused on empowering its citizens, particularly women and youth, through initiatives in education, healthcare, digitalization, and entrepreneurship.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
